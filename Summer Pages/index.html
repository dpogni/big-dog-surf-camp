Beginner & Intermediate Lessons - Ages 5 to 16

Big Dog Surf Camp is the premier surf camp for Marin and the North Bay Area. The summer camp is perfect for any young eager kids living in Marin County and San Francisco. We will provide the boards & wetsuits. Week long and single sessions are available. We will be surfing in Marin. Students must know how to swim and be ready to have a fun time.

Campers should be dropped off at Proof Lab (254 Shoreline Hwy, Mill Valley) and will be shuttled to the beach. The camp runs from 9am to 4pm.
