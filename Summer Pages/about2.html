Ian aka The Big Dog

Born and raised in Northern California, Ian has been surfing for 15 years. Surfing has led Ian to enter as many surf contests as he could get into, and continues to inspire his endeavors all around the world. While Ian's dreams of going pro came and went, his passion for the ocean never wavered. Ian graduated from Dominican University focusing on becoming a teacher. Ian currently subs at Ring Mountain Elementary and Middle School, and incorporates his love for teaching and surfing together by conducting a surf summer camp. Teaching is as much of a passion for him as surfing and being able to merge the two has been a dream come true.

Marty

Marty Magnussen - San Francisco's surfing king pin and Maverick's charger. Traveled all around the world hunting big waves and has had shots published in all the major surf mags. Also a certified EMT.

Cullen aka Coach Cully

Big Island king pin and sponsored pro. Big airs, big barrels and huge carves. Soccer guru and a true gentleman.

Jay Hurd aka Coach Jay

One of the best surfers ever to come out of Marin. Been getting shots in mags since he was 15! Charged some of the heaviest waves around the world. Recently graced us with the cover of the IJ weekend section (july 17th). Remarkable real estate agent as well.

Robin aka Coach Sweetness

19 yr-old wonder kid. Lives between here and the Gold Coast of Australia. Phenomenal skater and surfer with a pretty face to match.

Ian Ross aka Coach Raw Dog

Artist/wave magnet. Lived up and down the California coast perfecting his style in and out of the water. High school surf partner of the Big Dog. Soccer/skating/surfing/painting maestro.
